How StartAI works

Selecting an AI tool

Begin by choosing the right AI tool for your needs. Consider the specific objectives and requirements of your project or task (write social media ads, hero sections, blog posts...).

Set Content Parameters

Define the content parameters such as the desired length, tone, and style of your content. This helps the AI tailor the output to your specific requirements.

Initiate Content Generation

Click the 'Generate' button or initiate the content generation process in your chosen AI tool. The AI will use your keywords and parameters to create the content.

Review and Refine

Review the generated content, and make any necessary refinements or edits to ensure it meets your standards and objectives.
