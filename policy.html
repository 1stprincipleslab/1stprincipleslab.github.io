1. Introduction

At 1st Principles Lab (“we”, “us”, “our”), we are committed to protecting the privacy of individuals and organizations who interact with us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you use our website or engage with our AI-based development services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, company name, job title, country of operation
  • Service-Related Data: Data shared with us for project delivery such as technical documents, business requirements, datasets, and APIs
  • Website Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device info, and pages visited
  • Cookies and Tracking: We use cookies and analytics tools to monitor site usage and improve performance

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries and deliver services
  • To customize and improve our AI-based solutions
  • To communicate service updates, insights, and newsletters
  • To promote our services using anonymized project descriptions or client names/logos (non-confidential only)
  • To meet our contractual and legal obligations

4. Marketing and Promotional Use

We may use your company name, logo, and a non-sensitive description of services provided for marketing purposes, including:

  • Website and portfolio showcases
  • Presentations and proposals to prospective clients
  • Public case studies or testimonials (only with your consent)

We will never disclose confidential information, project data, or results without prior written permission. You can opt out of such usage by emailing us at k2@1stprincipleslab.com.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share your data with:

  • Our employees and contractors bound by confidentiality agreements
  • Trusted service providers (e.g., CRM, hosting, analytics platforms) under data protection contracts
  • Legal authorities when required to comply with applicable laws

6. International Data Transfers

We may store or process your data in New Zealand, India, or other jurisdictions where we operate. We implement appropriate safeguards to protect your information across borders.

7. Data Security

We apply robust physical, administrative, and technical safeguards including:

  • Data encryption
  • Access control policies
  • Periodic audits and backups

8. Your Rights

You may request to:

  • Access or correct your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw your consent for non-essential uses

To exercise your rights, contact us at k2@1stprincipleslab.com.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for their content or data practices.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
